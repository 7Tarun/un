5 Proven Strategies That Shave Years Off Your Loans

1. The Offset Account Power-Play

Every $10,000 sitting in a 100% offset account saves you approximately $600–$800 per year in interest and cuts months off your loan.

2. Fortnightly Repayment Switch

Switching from monthly to fortnightly repayments = one extra monthly payment per year — automatically.

3. The Debt Cascade Method

Clear smaller, higher-interest debts first, then cascade those payments into your home loan for exponential speed.

4. Windfall & Bonus Blitz

Every tax return, work bonus or gift goes 100% to debt — clients who do this shave an extra 3–7 years.

5. Strategic Refinancing

A 0.30%–0.70% rate drop + better features can save $100,000+ over the life of a loan.
